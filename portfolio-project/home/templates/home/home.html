About Me

For as long as I can remember, I have always been victim to an ardent inquisitiveness regarding all realms of technology. When I was a child, I would break apart various devices and toys simply to disinter their internal workings and scrutinize their core mechanisms. Inevitably, as I grew older, the inquisitiveness became more potent and thus led me to explore different aspects of computer science and develop a meticulous understanding of its prominent facets, such as, software engineering, data analysis, web designing, app development, ethical hacking and game development.

Currently, I am pursuing my bachelor's in computer science at the Berea College in Kentucky where I have been awarded a full-tuition scholarship. Alongside programming for my classes, I indulge myself in consistently taking online courses related to software development and working on as many side-projects as I can to ensure that I implement in real life whatever I learn.

I plan on becoming a top of the notch software developer using programming to find viable solutions for existing problems.
